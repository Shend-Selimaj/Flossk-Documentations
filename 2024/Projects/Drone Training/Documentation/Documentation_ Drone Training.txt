Title: Drone Training

Project Description:
FLOSSK launched a Drone Training program to provide participants with hands-on experience in drone technology, focusing on safe operation, aerial photography, and relevant regulations. The program fostered collaboration, boosting participants' confidence in using drones, and many expressed interest in future training opportunities.

Activities:
Key activities:

Comprehensive training on drone operation and safety protocols
Practical skills in piloting drones and conducting aerial photography
Encouraged career interest in drone technology
Beneficiaries:
Students, hobbyists, and aspiring professionals gained practical drone experience and confidence, with many showing interest in further training.
