WoALUG 2020

Project Duration: December 2019 to December 2020

Project Description:
WoALUG 2020 aimed to empower youth by promoting open-source principles and fostering a collaborative environment in technology. The project focused on enhancing youth participation in tech initiatives and promoting intercultural cooperation.

Activities:

Hosted workshops on open-source software development.
Facilitated discussions on youth inclusion and intercultural cooperation.
Engaged youth in interactive activities for skill development and collaboration.
Beneficiaries:
Youth participants gained valuable skills in open-source technologies, cooperation, and reconciliation. Local community members benefited from shared learning and networking opportunities.

Partners:
Wikimedia Foundation
