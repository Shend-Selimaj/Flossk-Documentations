Prishtina Python Meetup
Date: December 28, 2020
Location: Online (meet.flossk.org)
Organizer: FLOSSK

Ylber Veliu, software engineer and creator of Për Programera, led a session on building and consuming REST APIs using Flask. Participants gained hands-on experience and engaged in discussions, with FLOSSK encouraging continued networking through their online community.

Open Hardware Meetup
Date: December 21, 2020
Location: Online (meet.flossk.org)
Organizer: FLOSSK & Prishtina Hackerspace

This panel discussion focused on the impact of open-source hardware in education and industry. Speakers included Agron Dida (Zgjoi.com), Dukagjin Rudi (FARA), Erdet Nasufi (Solaborate), and Besfort Guri (FLOSSK). The event, supported by the Municipality of Pristina, emphasized community-driven innovation.

Balkan FLOSStival 2020
Date: December 5, 2020
Location: Online
Organizers: FLOSSK, Open Labs, fossaegean, GFOSS, GreekLUG, SHARE Foundation

A regional celebration of free and open-source software, privacy, and open data. Sessions covered cybersecurity, Linux, and open-source applications for education and business. Sponsored by EU for Innovation, the event showcased collaboration across multiple organizations.

OSM GeoWeek 2020
Date: November 17-18, 2020
Location: Online
Organizer: FLOSSK

FLOSSK partnered with the University of Pristina’s Geography, Geodesy, and Architecture departments for a panel discussion and a mapathon. Participants contributed to OpenStreetMap by mapping roads, transit stations, and essential infrastructure, demonstrating the power of collective geographic data contributions.

DOKU.TECH 2020
Date: November 14, 2020
Location: Klan Kosova (Hybrid Event)
Organizer: FLOSSK

A hybrid event featuring discussions on technology’s role in public policy, software development, and innovation.
