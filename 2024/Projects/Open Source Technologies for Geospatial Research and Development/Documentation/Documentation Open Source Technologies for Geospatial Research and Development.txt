Title: Open Source Technologies for Geospatial Research and Development

Project Description:
The project aimed to equip students and teachers with modern, open-source geospatial tools and methodologies for geographical studies. It focused on updating the existing curriculum with the 'Geo for All' curriculum, including monthly activities and workshops. Students engaged in various research topics such as land use mapping, urbanization, deforestation, and flooding, enhancing their understanding of geographic concepts and developing critical, analytical, and imaginative thinking skills.

Activities:
Key activities:

Sourcing and setting up laboratory equipment for geospatial research
Recruiting participants from the Geography Department
Hosting bi-monthly workshops led by experts on data collection, mapping, remote sensing, and drone technologies
Conducting monthly meetups and mapathons at Prishtina Hackerspace
Beneficiaries:
Primary beneficiaries: Students and teaching staff of the University of Prishtina’s Geography Department.
Secondary beneficiaries: Wider community, public institutions, OSM & OSGeo local chapter members.

Partners:

University of Prishtina, Department of Geography
Project Duration:
06 January 2024 - 14 December 2024
