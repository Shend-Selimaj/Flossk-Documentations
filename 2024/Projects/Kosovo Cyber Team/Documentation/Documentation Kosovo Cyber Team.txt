Title: Kosovo Cyber Team

Project Description:
The Kosovo Cyber Team was a national initiative focused on advancing cybersecurity knowledge and skills in Kosovo by bringing together students, young professionals, and cybersecurity enthusiasts. The initiative promoted education, awareness, and innovation, providing hands-on experience through training, workshops, and competitions. The team represented Kosovo in international events such as the European Cybersecurity Challenge (ECSC), contributing to the growth of the country’s cybersecurity ecosystem. FLOSSK acted as the fiscal sponsor for this project.

Activities:
Key activities:

Organized hands-on workshops tackling cybersecurity challenges
Conducted multiple training sessions on various cybersecurity topics
Developed partnerships with local and international organizations
Held competitions to apply cybersecurity knowledge in practical settings
Beneficiaries:
Participants from universities and technical schools across Kosovo gained valuable skills and networking opportunities.

Project Duration:
August 2024 – December 2024
