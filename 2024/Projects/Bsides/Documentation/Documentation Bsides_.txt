Title: Bsides 2024

Project Description:
BSides Prishtina 2024 marked the third edition of the local BSides security conference in Kosovo, held at the University of Prishtina. The event aimed to foster collaboration among IT talent and security professionals, further developing the growing information security community in Kosovo.

Activities:
Key activities:

Hosted keynote presentations and panel discussions
Conducted specialized workshops led by industry experts
Organized a Capture-the-Flag (CTF) challenge for participants
Provided networking opportunities with local and international cybersecurity professionals
Beneficiaries:
The primary beneficiaries were IT students, aspiring cybersecurity professionals, and established practitioners in Kosovo’s information security community.

Project Duration:
May 2023 - May 2024
