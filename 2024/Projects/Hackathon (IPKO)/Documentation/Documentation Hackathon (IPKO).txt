Title: Hackathon (IPKO)

Project Description:
The "IoT for Green Transition" hackathon at ITP Prizren brought together students from AUK Kosovo, FIEK, and UPZ to address environmental challenges using IoT technology. This event promoted innovation, skill development, and networking among young tech enthusiasts, fostering sustainable tech solutions to drive Kosovo's green transition.

Activities:
Key activities:

Coordinated with university partners to align on event goals
Opening ceremony followed by team formation
Mentorship sessions with Enis Hyseni, Besfort Guri, and Lis Luci
Coding sessions and scheduled breaks
Presentation and judging of solutions based on innovation, impact, and feasibility
Award ceremony recognizing the top three teams with prizes (€1500, €800, €600)
Beneficiaries:
Over 100 students showcased their skills, learned from experts, and collaborated on environmental sustainability solutions.

Project Duration:
May 23rd - May 25th, 2024
