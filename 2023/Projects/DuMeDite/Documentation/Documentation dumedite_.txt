Title: DuMeDite Platform (2023)

Project Description:
DuMeDite.org is an online platform designed to enhance public transparency in Kosovo by facilitating easy FOI requests. It aligns with Kosovo's Law on Access to Public Documents, enabling citizens to submit requests and access responses from public institutions.

Activities:

Research phase identifying public needs regarding transparency.
Baseline analysis on legal frameworks and platform integration.
Focus groups for stakeholder feedback.
Pre/post-test evaluations of citizens’ platform awareness.
Training sessions for CSOs and journalists on platform use.
Platform maintenance and continued operational support post-project.
Beneficiaries:
Kosovar citizens, CSOs, journalists, public institutions.

Partners:

National Endowment for Democracy (NED).

Project Duration:
October 1, 2022 – December 31, 2023
